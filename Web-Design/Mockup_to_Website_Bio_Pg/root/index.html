About

Kedice Jones is a second-year media student at the University of Guelph - Humber. At her university, she is the Vice – President of GHAMA and will be its next president for the 2018/2019 year. She plans to graduate with a bachelor of applied arts from the University of Guelph and a diploma in media business from Humber College. She hopes to get an MBA to advance her skill and find her dream job.
In addition to her occupation, she enjoys learning new things related to business, media, design and lifestyle habits. She’s always willing to help others with their school task and eager to take on challenges. Her top apps on her iPhone are Pinterest, Twitter, Instagram, and the podcast app, where she’s always up to date on the latest memes and the media market.
