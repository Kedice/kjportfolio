About

My name is Kedice Jones and I am a diligent media studies student specializing in media business at the University of Guelph – Humber. From the start of my academic career at the University of Guelph – Humber I have been active in the Guelph – Humber Advertising & Marketing Association, moving from being a member, to a Design Executive to current President in the past three years. During that time the roles that I was in required me to create visuals and come up with strategies for advertising for events.

Outside from Guelph - Humber’s societies I have also managed to accrue nearly three years of work experience. I have the privilege of being a sales associate at Lindt & Sprüngli along with being a Media Coordinator for the company Above & Beyond Home Inspector and Our Women’s Voices organization this past summer. At Lindt & Sprüngli I have learned the importance of being a positive and knowledgeable representation of a brand. This representation consists of me being informed of new sales or products, finding the best solutions for customers and leaving customers a positive, memorable experience. At Above & Beyond Home Inspector, I gained valuable professional skills such as data entry and records management through the task that was assigned to me. With the Our Women’s Voices organization I became well knowledgeable about the importance of diversity and representation online. Although professional skills are essential in the communication field having a sensitive insight of how things can be perceived from a different group of people is critical a will for having successful, engaging posts and received messages from targeting audience. Whether working on educational, extracurricular, or professional projects, my supervisors and peers praise me for my analytical, strategy and creative thinking skills, which I hope to leverage into any communication related internship. By the way, check out my awesome work.
